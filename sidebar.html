Applying for a PhD

Dr. Edwin Simpson welcomes applications from very strong candidates to the CDT in Practice-oriented AI, the Data-driven Engineering and Sciences PhD programme, University scholarships, or other scholarship schemes such as the China scholarship council. If these are a good fit for you, you can send your CV and a description of your research interests to Edwin by email, and find further information about applications from the links above.
